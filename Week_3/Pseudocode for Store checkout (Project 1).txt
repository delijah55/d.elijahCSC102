Pseudocode for Store checkout after Purchase:


Scan Items at checkout

Calculate the total amount of the purchase.

Ask the customer if they have discounts to apply.

If a discount is provided, apply it to the total amount.

Calculate the final amount to be paid after applying the discount.

Ask the customer to choose a payment method.

Process the payment according to the chosen method.

Print a receipt with the details of the purchase, including the total amount, any discounts applied,The final amount paid, and the payment method used.
