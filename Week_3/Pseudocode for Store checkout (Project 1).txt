Pseudocode for Store checkout after Purchase:

1) Calculate the total amount of the purchase.

2) Ask the customer if they have discounts to apply.

3) If a discount is provided, apply it to the total amount.

4) Calculate the final amount to be paid after applying the discount.

5) Ask the customer to choose a payment method.

6) Process the payment according to the chosen method.

7) Print a receipt with the details of the purchase, including the total amount, any discounts applied.

8) The final amount paid, and the payment method used.
